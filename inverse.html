Perspective

is the technique

used in art, drawing, painting

that is used to create

the illusion

of three-dimensions

depth and space

in a two-dimensional

flat surface

this is used to create space that is not real

how can we revolutionize perspective?

in the digital medium1

the conflation between 2D and 3D

manufactures synthetic space

using perspective in a new way

where traditional drawing falls short

digital space

redefines what is real space

virtual reality

exists through the artistic

perspective to infinity

focus on the Z-AXIS

illustrate the possibility for digital construction

of meaning
